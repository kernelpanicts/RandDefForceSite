About The RADF

The RADF or "Rando Defense Force" is a GTA Online Crew run by KernelKettle and Quack who's purpose is to help normal players have the ability to enjoy GTA Online lobbies without constantly being Blown-up by so called "Tryhards", or people who only play to grief low-level players. While this is not a foolproof solution, it will at least direct attention away from normal players to our guys. We have a few groups with unique ways to do this:

  • Navy
  • Ground Forces
  • Air Force
  • Special Ordnance

The Responsibilities of each group will be presented below.

■ Navy (RDNV)

The Naval Division operates primarily from the Kosatka Submarine using the guided missiles. The Naval Commander is Commissioner Quack.

■ Ground Forces (RDGF)

The Ground Forces Division operates on land in vehicles and on foot to take down hostile forces in any location. They are for those who want to be in the action and go guns-a-blazing, or run people over. The Ground Force Commander is Lieutenant CurryBeast.

■ Air Force (RDAF)

The Air Force Division operates in the air in Helicopters, Planes, Jets, and Bombers to eliminate hostiles and divert attention away from the Ground Force units. They will need to be able to use flares and evasive manuvers to avoid homing missiles and other fighters in the air. The Interim Air Force Commander is Commissioner Quack.

■ Special Ordnance (RDSO)

The Special Ordnance Division operates everywhere with any playstyle they want. They Have at least moderate experience in everything and are trusted by all the division leaders. The Special Ordnance Division can use anything to ensure a job gets done. The Special Ordnance Commander is KernelKettle.
